Examples of Previous DoS Attacks

Morris Worm (1988)

  • Description: One of the earliest examples of a denial of service, though not a traditional DoS attack.
  • Impact: Infected approximately 10% of the internet at the time, causing widespread disruptions.
  • Method: Exploited vulnerabilities in Unix systems to spread and overload systems.

SYN Flood Attack on Panix (1996)

  • Description: Panix, an ISP, was targeted by a SYN flood attack.
  • Impact: Overwhelmed Panixâ€™s servers with fake SYN requests, causing a 36-hour downtime.
  • Method: Sent numerous SYN requests to initiate connections without completing them, exhausting server resources.

MafiaBoy Attacks (2000)

  • Description: Michael "Mafiaboy" Calce, a teenager, launched DDoS attacks against major websites.
  • Impact: Temporarily shut down websites like Yahoo!, Amazon, eBay, and CNN, causing significant disruption.
  • Method: Used a tool called TFN2 to coordinate attacks using previously infected computers to generate massive amounts of traffic.

GitHub DoS Attack (2018)

  • Description: GitHub faced one of the largest recorded DDoS attacks.
  • Impact: Traffic peaked at 1.35 Tbps, causing temporary service disruptions
  • Method: Used a memcached amplification technique to generate a large volume of traffic.
